These projects are very minimal in nature, and not intended to create music for listening purposes, but as a demonstration, to show the possibilities and potential of interactive music. They were heavily inspired by the music and ideas of Iannis Xenakis

⇛ Ostinato Snake
⇛ Cluster Brickbreaker
⇛ Harmonic Tetris
Classic video games, with an "interactive soundtrack" i.e. music determined by what is happening in the game, and how it is being played.
⇛ Solar System
⇛ State Change
⇛ 0 and 1
Controllable simulations of "potential" soundscapes, i.e. systems where the energy created lacks the physical criteria to become actual sound.
⇛ fear_factory
⇛ memory_palace
⇛ zen_garden
The definition of instrument here is very broad - referring to any kind of interface you can use to make sound in a deterministic way.
